RFID-Based Attendance System

Automatic attendance using RFID cards or student number. Simplifying student record management at WMSU.

What is RFID?

Technology behind automated attendance tracking

RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) is a technology using cards or tags to automatically scan information.

Students tap their RFID ID card, and the system records attendance automatically — no manual signing required.

How It Works

A simple four-step process for attendance

1

Setup Stage

Create student database & assign RFID cards.

2

Input Stage

Students tap RFID card or input student number.

3

Processing Stage

System verifies ID, checks schedule, records attendance.

4

Report Stage

Teachers can view daily & monthly attendance reports.
